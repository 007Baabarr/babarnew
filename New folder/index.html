Guess the Number Game

How to Play

Guess the secret number correctly within a range of 1 to 100.

You have a total of 10 rounds to play.

To win the game, you must successfully guess the number correctly in at least 3 out of the 10 rounds.

Game Rules

In each round, you will be asked to guess a number between 1 and 100.

If your guessed number matches the secret number, you win that round.

The game will keep track of the number of rounds won.

Game Outcome

Winning: If you win at least 3 rounds out of 10, you win the game!

Losing: If you win fewer than 3 rounds after 10 rounds of play, you lose the game.
